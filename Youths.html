SIGN LANGUAGE FOR PRIMARY STUDENTS AND YOUTH

"GOODBYE"

"Goodbye" in American Sign Language(ASL)is signed by waving your dominant hand plam-outward in front of your body,moving it from left to right in a downward arc.

"SORRY"

"Sorry" is made by forming an 'A'with your right handon your chest using acouple of clockwise motions.This sign can also be used to mean 'apologize' or 'regret'.

"YES"

"Yes" in American Sign Language(ASL),is typically made by closing your hand into a fist and moving it up and down slightly, usually in front of your chest or chin.
