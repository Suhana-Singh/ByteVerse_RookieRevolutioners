SIGN LANGUAGE FOR TODDLERS

"PLEASE"

Please in American Sign Language(ASL) is signed by placing your flat right hand over your chest and moving it in circular motion.It's a polite way to request something or to show courtesy.

"HELLO"

Hello in American Sign Language(ASL) used especially by Mother's to teach toddler's,mother could use a sign that incorporates both a greeting and maternal touch.One way to do this is to sign"hello" with your non-dominant hand,symbolizing warmth and concern.

"GREETING"

Greeting in American Sign Language(ASL) used by Father's to teach their toddler's involves tapping your forehead twice with the thumb of your open hand.This motion symbolizes the idea of "thinking of"or"remembering" your father.
