SIGN LANGUAGE FOR SENIOR CITIZEN

"OKAY"

Okay in American Sign Language(ASL) used to describe something having gone through approval process and having received an okay.It involves folding the four fingers except thumb and pointing the thumb upwards.

"GOOD"

Good in American Sign Language(ASL) is signed by placing the fingers of your right hand against your lips.Move your right hand into the palm of your left hand.Both hand should be facing upward.

"HAPPY"

Happy in American Sign Language(ASL) involves raising both hands with the index fingers extended,curling them into hooks,and bringing them up towards the cheeks while smiling.It's a beautiful expression of joy and contentment
