Information related to sign language:

What is Sign Language?
Sign language is a visual-gestural language used by deaf and hard-of-hearing people to communicate. In India, the Indian Sign Language (ISL) is predominantly used, which has its own syntax, grammar, and vocabulary

Indian Sign Language Research and Training Center (ISLRTC)
ISLRTC is an autonomous body under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD), Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, Government of India. It works towards the research, development, and promotion of Indian Sign Language.

Sign Language Interpreter
A sign language interpreter is a professional who facilitates communication between deaf or hard-of-hearing individuals and hearing individuals by translating spoken language into sign language and vice versa.
